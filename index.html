Consulting

Experimental Programming

Design and coding of highly precise and user-friendly experiments (online & offline; using Python/ Psychopy, MATLAB, SR-Research...).

Project Management

Data manipulation, visualization, analysis, and sharing. I use mostly R & tidyverse with git version control.

Edition and Revision

Revise and edit scientific manuscripts and reports. Portuguese-English translation.

I am a psychologist broadly interested in learning and language. I am passionate about parsimonious explanations and inventive methods. I am constantly working with experimental design, data managment, visualization, and analyses. I strive to do so following open science best practices. Whenever possible, I use open software and version controlled repositories that allows me to openly share my work in a reproducible way (R, Python, github, OSF...). I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Concordia Infant Research Lab, Montreal. Here, I work with open science in infant reseach and large-scale collaborations. Next winter (January/2022), I am moving to Ambrose University, Calgary, as an Assistant Professor. Please feel free to contact me and check my cv.

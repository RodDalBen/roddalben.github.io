I am a psychologist broadly interested in learning and language. I am passionate about parsimonious explanations and inventive methods. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Concordia Infant Research Lab, Montreal. I work with open science in infant reseach and large-scale collaborations. Next winter (January/2022), I am moving to Ambrose University, Calgary, as an Assistant Professor. Feel free to contact me.
